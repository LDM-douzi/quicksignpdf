Privacy Policy

Papertrail PDF Editor is designed to process documents in the browser. Uploaded PDFs and edits are intended to stay on the user's device unless the product later introduces cloud features.

How the editor works

The editor runs client-side in the browser. It can temporarily store session data in local browser storage so work can be restored after refresh.

Information we may process

  • PDF files you choose to open in the browser.
  • Edits, signatures, images, and annotations you add while using the editor.
  • Basic hosting analytics or server logs, depending on the hosting platform you use.

Local storage

The app may store your active session locally in the browser so you can recover work after a refresh. Clearing browser storage may remove that saved session.

Third-party hosting

If this site is hosted on platforms such as Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare, those providers may collect technical request data under their own policies.
